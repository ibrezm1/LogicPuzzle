How to Play

1

Each puzzle level starts with the black starter blocks already placed and locked in their correct positions on the board. You cannot move or lift locked blocks.

2

Drag the remaining colored blocks from the inventory onto the empty slots of the 8x8 grid.

3

The goal is to pack the board completely with zero empty cells and zero overlaps!

Controls:

  • Drag & Drop: Drag blocks directly to place them on the grid.
  • Rotate: Press the 'R' key, Right-Click while dragging, or use the Rotate button.
  • Flip: Press the 'F' key or use the Flip button.
  • Lift/Return: Click or tap a placed piece on the board to lift it back to the inventory.

Level Cleared!

Excellent spatial reasoning! You packed the grid perfectly.
